Jagannath International Management School (JIMS) Library is an invaluable resource for students, researchers and faculties of Management, Mass Communication and Information Technology. The JIMS library was found in the year 2003. The library has over the years built a robust collection of over 12724 books, over 46 journals (including 12 International journals), Over 19 Magazines, 6 newspapers, Student's Project Reports, Economic Surveys Reports, Subject Manuals, Solved End Term Papers, Best Answer Sheets of Mid Term Papers and many other resources. The library also provides access to the digital resources through university network subscription to various databases.
