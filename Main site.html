About Jims

Established in 2003, JIMS, Vasant Kunj, New Delhi, has emerged as one of the topmost institutes of GGSIPU in Delhi in just over a decade. The college has been awarded the highest grade, 'A', by the Joint Assessment Committee of GGS Indraprastha University, the Govt. of NCT of Delhi, and by the University Academic Audit Committee. It has also been awarded 'A+', the highest grade, by the State Fee Regulatory Committee of the Govt. of NCT of Delhi for the last five years. The institute has also been awarded 'A+' Grade by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council, Bangalore.
